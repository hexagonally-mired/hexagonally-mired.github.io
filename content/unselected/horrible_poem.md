# Why is Yellow Green?

![original image](/images/poem.avif)

Why is yellow green? 

Yellow's creativity is just bubbling,

so when I sit down to reimagine spring, 

why is yellow green?


I want yellow to be yellow so my heart can be mellow,

and so my mind does bellow, 

HONESTLY, PLEASE JUST BE YELLOW!


Not gold, orange, or flaxen. 

Yellow is yellow & 

I wish people wouldn't hate it so


Oh, I'm not done. 

Sometimes yellow is whitened,

and so my heart tightens

I wish it wasn’t that bright,

So I could use it at night.


Yellow is yellow

Blue is blue

But my heart is red

So I’ll just cry in bed.
