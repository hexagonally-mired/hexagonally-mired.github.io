# Music Recs!!!

## Morgan Nagler

if you've got nothing to listen to on your way back, and the time is past 9p, I think you'll be able to listen to Morgan Nagler's 'I've Got Nothing To Lose, and I'm Losing It'
the full album releases at midnight Eastern Daylight, and (at least on spotify) it drops at 2100.

She co-wrote Phoebe Bridgers' Kyoto, and the songs from the album she's already dropped are super fun
