# Can We Have Open-Source AI Hosting on Personal Servers?

* If so, why hasn't it caught on?

* If not, why not?

People have lately been very concerned with artificial intelligence servers' overuse of water. It's a very real concern, and one that I really hope we can solve.

If it could, we'd have so much freer customization and better use of AI.
